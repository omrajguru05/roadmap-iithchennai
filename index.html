ROADMAP :- IIT CHENNAI

After clearing the JEE Advanced exam, the process of admission into IIT Chennai involves several steps:

1. JEE Advanced Exam:

  • You need to qualify for the JEE Advanced exam by meeting the cutoff marks set by the conducting authority.

2. Counseling and Seat Allocation:

  • Candidates who qualify for JEE Advanced are eligible for counseling conducted by the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA).
  • During counseling, you'll have the opportunity to select your preferred IITs and courses based on your rank and availability of seats.

3. Choice Filling and Locking:

  • You'll need to fill in your choices of institutes and courses in the order of preference during the counseling process.
  • After selecting your choices, make sure to lock them within the specified time frame.

4. Seat Allotment:

  • Based on your JEE Advanced rank, choices filled, and availability of seats, you'll be allotted a seat in one of the IITs, including IIT Chennai.

5. Reporting to Allotted Institute:

  • Once you're allocated a seat in IIT Chennai, you need to report to the institute within the specified time frame.
  • Complete the admission formalities and pay the required fees to secure your seat.

Score Required for IIT Chennai:

  • The score required to get into IIT Chennai varies from year to year and depends on factors like the difficulty level of the exam and the number of applicants.
  • Generally, to secure admission into top IITs like IIT Chennai, you'll need to aim for a high JEE Advanced rank, preferably within the top few thousand ranks.

  • Other Specialized Courses:

    • Tuition Fees: Around ₹2 lakhs per year.
